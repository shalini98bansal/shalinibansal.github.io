01 / About

I'm a mechanical engineer at Haas Automation in Oxnard, where I develop CNC test parts end-to-end — from GD&T-driven CAD in Fusion 360, through 5-axis machining and inspection, to the automated regression scripts that validate every new machine feature against engineering requirements.

My background spans aerospace FEA on aircraft-engine blisks, research on a steerable robotic catheter for cardiovascular surgery at UCR, and four years of multidisciplinary team design on a human-powered recumbent racer.
